ABOUT THE CHAPTER

Elevating Ideas into Legacy, at UCSC

Established in 2025 by the University of Colombo School of Computing (UCSC), The CSSL GenZ Chapter of UCSC stands as an initiative dedicated to empowering the next generation of IT professionals. As a proud extension of the Computer Society of Sri Lanka (CSSL), our chapter serves as a place for innovation and continuous learning. We aim to bridge the gap between academia and industry, offering our members unique opportunities to enhance their expertise, leadership skills, and professional networks. With a clear vision for the future, we are committed to driving an impactful change in the field of computing.
