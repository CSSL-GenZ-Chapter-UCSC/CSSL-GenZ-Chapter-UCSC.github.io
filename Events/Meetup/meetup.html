In Person Meetup

27 Tue, May 15:00 - 17:00 W002, UCSC

The CSSL GenZ Chapter of UCSC hosted its first physical meetup, marking an exciting and energetic beginning. The evening brought together chapter members for an opportunity of interaction and lighthearted competition.

What began with formal introductions quickly transformed into an atmosphere filled with laughter, games, and fun.

This memorable evening not only broke the ice among members but also laid the foundation for a series of initiatives to come.
