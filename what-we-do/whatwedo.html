We Just Do These

Competitions

Compete with the Best, to Become the Best. At the CSSL GenZ Chapter of UCSC, we don't just organize competitions, we create platforms for excellence.

Whether you're here to prove your skills or to sharpen them, this is your chance to learn, grow, and showcase your talent.

Events and Activities

At the CSSL GenZ Chapter of UCSC, it's more than just tech. It's about people, purpose, and passion. We offer volunteering opportunities that allow you to gain a new exposure, develop soft skills, and build connections, all while having fun and making a meaningful impact.

Member Development

At the CSSL GenZ Chapter of UCSC we believe in growing together. We create an environment where members are encouraged to develop in their respective fields, supported by a culture of collaboration, coexistence, and shared success.

Workshops

Workshops are conducted to deepen your knowledge in specific tech domains and build you with the skills needed to stay ahead. Led by experts and experienced peers, these sessions help students become tech ready, confident, and capable in their chosen fields.
