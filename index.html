ABOUT THE CHAPTER

Transforming bytes into brilliance

Established in 2021 by the Faculty of Computing, we take immense pride in being the first-ever Student Chapter of the Computer Society of Sri Lanka, paving the way for a new era of IT professionalism and development among our undergraduates. Our chapter is a dynamic platform for aspiring IT professionals, providing unparalleled personal and professional growth opportunities. With a commitment to fostering knowledge, skills, and industry connections, the CSSL GenZ Chapter of KDU has become a catalyst for excellence in information technology.
