Empowering Tomorrow's Technologists: Launching the CSSL Gen Z Chapter at UCSC

UCSC CSSL GenZ Launch Event

Official Launch of the CSSL GenZ Chapter of UCSC

16 Wed, July
16:00 - 18:00
New Arts Theatre, University of Colombo

We are pleased to announce the Launching Ceremony of the CSSL GenZ Chapter at the University of Colombo School of Computing (UCSC). This event marks the inauguration of an initiative dedicated to the next generation of tech leaders. The CSSL GenZ Chapter aims to facilitate innovation, leadership, and collaboration among students, aligning with the broader vision of the Computer Society of Sri Lanka. Event Details Date: Wednesday, 16th July 2025 Time: 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM Venue: New Arts Theatre, UOC This launch signifies a new chapter, one driven by the collective potential of our UCSC student community.
