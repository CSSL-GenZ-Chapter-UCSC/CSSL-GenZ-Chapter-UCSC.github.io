The CSSL Research Colloquium 2025, organized by the Computer Society of Sri Lanka, is a nationally recognized platform that celebrates innovative research and development in the field of computing. Held as a key segment of the National IT Conference (NITC), the colloquium provides students the opportunity to present their original ideas, prototypes, and research to a panel of academic and industry experts.

This year, five outstanding undergraduate projects have been selected from the University of Colombo School of Computing (UCSC) to represent the institution at this prestigious event.

At the colloquium, the selected students will pitch their research or product ideas to a professional audience, gain valuable feedback, and stand a chance to be recognized at the national level. The platform also serves as a unique networking opportunity, connecting young innovators with leaders from academia, industry, and government.

We are proud to support our students as they take their ideas beyond the classroom, showcasing not only their technical capabilities but also the innovation that defines UCSC.
