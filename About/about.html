ABOUT CSSL

Transforming bytes into brilliance

In 2017, the CSSL organized Sri Lanka's flagship first International ICT event which saw 50 international delegates in addition to a large-scale platform which included the National Assembly of the SEARCC Conference, NITC and CSSLABS.

CSSL organizes Tech Talks every month for discussion and knowledge sharing on latest technologies among ICT professionals. The CPD Forum is organized for the same purpose but on a monthly basis which includes Information Officers and Heads of IT. The CSSL is working on the internationalization project under IT Mastermind to a TV Quiz program that the CSSL is popularizing ICT among school children and masses.

ABOUT CSSL GENZ CHAPTER AT UCSC

Transforming bytes into brilliance

The GenZ Chapter of the Computer Society of Sri Lanka (CSSL) at the General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University (KDU), established in 2021 by the Faculty of Computing, marking a significant milestone as the university's inaugural student chapter under the CSSL umbrella. This pioneering initiative has been designed to offer undergraduates valuable opportunities for personal and professional development in the field of Information Technology (IT).

Since its establishment, the CSSL GenZ Chapter at KDU has consistently contributed to the cultivation of IT professionalism among its members. The chapter plays a crucial role in providing a conducive environment for knowledge exchange, fostering essential skills, delivering industry insights, and creating direct linkages to the IT industry. The CSSL GenZ Chapter actively serves as a platform for IT professionalism by engaging students in various activities and events. These initiatives not only enhance members' academic learning but also facilitate the understanding of the IT landscape and establish valuable connections within the industry.

Under the guidance of Dr. Nirosha Wedasinghe, who serves as the Chapter Patron and a council member, the CSSL GenZ Chapter at General Sir John Kotelawala Defence University continues to strengthening and enthusiastically contribute to the development of IT professionalism among its student members.
