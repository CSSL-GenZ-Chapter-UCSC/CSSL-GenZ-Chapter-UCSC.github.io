ABOUT CSSL

The Computer Society of Sri Lanka

The Computer Society of Sri Lanka (CSSL) is the national body representing ICT professionals in Sri Lanka. Since 1976, CSSL has been at the forefront of promoting professionalism, ethical standards, and innovation in the ICT sector

CSSL is a member of SEARCC, IFIP, and OPA, and plays a key role both locally and internationally. It organizes major events such as the National IT Conference (NITC), National Schools Software Competition (NSSC), Tech Talks, and the CSSL ICT Awards, while also supporting startups, social initiatives, and digital policy advocacy

With a strong focus on empowering youth through its GenZ Chapter, CSSL is committed to building the next generation of tech leaders, driven by collaboration, diversity, and excellence.

ABOUT CSSL GENZ CHAPTER AT UCSC

Transforming bytes into brilliance

The CSSL GenZ Chapter at the University of Colombo School of Computing (UCSC) is a student-led initiative established in 2025 under the Computer Society of Sri Lanka (CSSL). As UCSC's CSSL affiliated student chapter, we are committed to empowering the next generation of IT professionals through innovation and industry aligned excellence.

Guided by Dr. Roshan Rajapaksha, our Chapter Patron and a visionary in computing education, the GenZ Chapter serves as a transformative platform for UCSC undergraduates. With Dr. Manjusri Wickramasinghe as our Senior Treasurer, we ensure a strong foundation in both leadership and governance.

In an era defined by rapid technological advancement, our chapter equips students with the skills, connections, and mindset to thrive. From research discussions to innovative projects, we create an ecosystem where GenZ minds can practice excellence.

Shape your future in tech alongside UCSC's brightest minds. If you're ready to learn and leave your mark, the CSSL GenZ Chapter at UCSC is where your journey begins.
